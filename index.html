All Purpose Shrinker - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy Last Updated: July 20, 2026 Privacy Policy for All Purpose Shrinker All Purpose Shrinker is developed by Dragoncore Entertainment. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Information We Collect All Purpose Shrinker is designed to process your files directly on your device. We do not collect, store, or upload: Your photos or files The contents of compressed files Personal information such as your name or email address EXIF metadata removed from your images All file compression and metadata removal are performed locally on your device. Advertising To keep the app free, All Purpose Shrinker may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services. These advertising providers may collect limited information such as: Device type Operating system version Approximate location (based on IP address) Advertising identifiers Information about ad views and interactions This information is collected by the advertising provider, not by Dragoncore Entertainment. It is used to display relevant advertisements, measure advertising performance, and prevent fraud. Permissions Depending on your Android version, the app may request permission to access files or photos so you can choose files to compress. These permissions are used only to perform the requested compression or metadata removal and are not used to collect your personal data. How We Use Information We use information only to: Compress your files. Remove EXIF metadata from images (if you choose). Improve the app's stability and performance. Display advertisements that help support the app. Data Security Because your files are processed on your device, they are not transmitted to our servers. We take reasonable steps to protect any information used by the app, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. Children's Privacy All Purpose Shrinker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Third-Party Services The app may use third-party services such as advertising providers. These services have their own privacy policies and are responsible for the information they collect. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on the app's listing.
